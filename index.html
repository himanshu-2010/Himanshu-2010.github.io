Smart Farm Automation System

IoT-enabled farm monitoring and control system designed to help farmers manage their agricultural operations remotely, addressing the real-world challenge of farmers needing to travel while keeping their farms monitored.

Core Features:

  • Environmental Monitoring: Temperature, humidity, and soil moisture sensors for real-time farm condition tracking
  • Automated Control: Smart irrigation pumps and lighting systems that activate based on sensor readings
  • Remote Access: Web-based interface accessible from anywhere in the world
  • Real-time Alerts: Notifications when conditions require attention

Impact:

This solution directly addresses the dilemma farmers face when they need to leave for purchasing supplies. With remote monitoring and control, they gain peace of mind and operational flexibility, leading to better crop management and reduced losses.
